GAMEPLAY

Ludo King is a digital version of the classic board game where the goal is to move all four of your tokens from the starting area to your home column before your opponents.

Basic Rules

  • Rolling the Dice: Players take turns rolling a single dice. You must roll a 6 to move a token out of the base onto the starting square CrazyGames
  • Token Movement: Click or tap a token to move it forward according to the dice roll. Tokens move along the board path toward the home column kingludo.com
  • Token Movement: Click or tap a token to move it forward according to the dice roll. Tokens move along the board path toward the home column
  • Capturing Opponents: Landing on a square occupied by an opponent’s token sends their token back to the base, and you get another dice roll
  • Safe Zones: Squares marked with a star are safe; tokens on these squares cannot be captured
  • Blocking: Two tokens of the same player on one square form a block that other players cannot pass

Winning the Game

  • Home Column: Tokens must enter the home column using the exact dice roll
  • Victory: The first player to move all four tokens into the home area wins the game

Tips and Strategies

  • Use 6s Wisely: Prioritize moving tokens out of the base and advancing them strategically
  • Block Opponents: Form blocks to prevent opponents from passing
  • Safe Zones: Plan moves to land on safe squares to avoid being sent back
  • Balance Risk and Reward: Decide when to advance tokens aggressively or keep them protected

Playing Online

Select Online Multiplayer, choose the number of players, and click PLAY. The game will match you with other players. Create a private room and invite friends to join for a multiplayer experience.
