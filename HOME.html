The Component of Ludo King

The key Components are the virtual game board

The game is design for casual play with friends or family online or on the same device.it is divided into squares forming a cross-shaped path and the dice to determine how far players move. Each player has four tokens that must travel from their starting area to the home area. Additional components such as the interface that displays the dice rolls, players orders. (more)
